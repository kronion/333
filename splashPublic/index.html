You're scrolling through days of old posts trying to find that fascinating article about mantis shrimp that you stumbled across on Facebook the other day because you know your date is really into marine life. You're trying to impress her, but you can't remember who shared it originally and now you just look weird because you swear it was a really cool article but you just can't find it anymore. Today's social networks relentlessly push new data which bury old, valuable content below hundreds of GIFs and click-bait articles. With 'Chive, you can keep track of the videos, pictures, and news that are important to you and share it with your friends in one place. 'Chive archives and organizes your content, making it easy to connect concepts or search in the future. Instead of going through all the boring statuses of “friends” you barely know, you can choose to follow certain people and the read articles that interest them. Use it privately as a smart replacement for bookmarks or publically as a forum for discussion of the latest news!
